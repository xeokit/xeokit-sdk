Gaussian Splatting — dirty PoC

Render-only port of the xeokit-sdk-v3 3DGS pipeline

Loads a compact .splat file (antimatter15 layout) and renders it with an instanced EWA splat pass registered as a standalone transparent drawable. No SceneModel, no picking, no section planes — just pixels, on a base that extends to the real thing.

Note: imports Viewer from the prebuilt dist/ bundle and GaussianSplatLoader straight from src/ (no build step needed).
